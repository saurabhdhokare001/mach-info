Pharmaceutical Machine Information

1. Syrup Making Machine

Syrup making machines are used to produce liquid formulations such as sugar syrups, cough syrups, and fruit-based syrups. These machines are designed to ensure accurate blending, heating, filtration, and storage of the liquid before bottling or packaging.

Main Features

  • Stainless steel tanks to avoid contamination.
  • Agitators for uniform mixing.
  • Heating systems for dissolving sugar or active ingredients.
  • Cooling jackets to maintain stability of the final product.
  • Filtration units to remove impurities before packaging.

Applications

Used in pharmaceutical industries for cough syrups, food industries for flavored syrups, and beverage industries for concentrates.
